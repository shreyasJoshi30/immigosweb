About Us

Feel isolated in Australian daily life? Immigos can help you feel connected.

Immigos can play multiple roles of your life. You can get news quickly, search locations of different types of facilities in Victoria and care about your health while being isolated at home by COVID-19!

Immigos 是一款多功能应用，他不仅能让你了解到最近的新闻，也能帮你找到你所向往的不同去处，还能在疫情时期时刻关注你的身体状况！

  • Events updating regularly. 活动事件定期更新
  • Willing to chat with you. 期待与你的交流

Frequently Asked Questions

Below lists some of problems you will meet with

  • How can I chat with the Chatbot? 如何与机器人交谈？

    You can freely enter what you want to ask, you can also just express your emotion. Immigos will give response anyway. 你可以畅所欲言，抒发情感，Immigos都会给予回应。

  • How can I change the language version? 如何切换语言版本？

    There are two buttons on the righthand on the top of page, the China button is Chinese version, the Australia button is English version. 在屏幕的右上角有两个按钮，中国的按钮对应中文版本，澳大利亚按钮对应英文版本。

  • Do I need to register for an account? 是否需要注册账户？

    Our application is totally free and open, and will charge nothing. Therefore there is no need to register and login. 我们的应用完全免费，所以并不需要注册和登录。

  • How can I find Asian stores? 我怎样寻找亚洲商店？

    We apply a specific function for you - just click on the Asian Store! 我们专门为您设计了相关功能，请点击亚洲商店！

  • What is Event about? 事件功能是做什么用的？

    We introduce some events around you and wish they can help you involve in Australian better and feel more social connected. 我们向您介绍周围的事件并期望它们能帮助您更好的适应澳大利亚的生活。
